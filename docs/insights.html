Publishing rule

Show work, do not teach juniors

The strongest content angle is not “how to write tests.” It is “how I turn quality problems into systems.” The audience is CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Product, Founder, or QA Director. They want confidence that production will not break, not a checklist tutorial.
